The Premium Paradise Institue of Etiquette Summer programs Program brings to your child an exclusive opportunity. We took our comprehensive curriculum and designed an exclusive-limited summer program to provide your child with a nice introduction to the basics of respect and good manners. Your childs day will be filled with hands on activities, practical practice and idea sharing. On the next page you will see an overview of topics covered in each day of the summer camp which includes a 15-20 break for snacks.

As part of our Summer Camp Program, each participant, will receive:

A Premium Paradise Institue of Etiquette workbook to take home. It is filled with fun activities chosen to support the information they receive in class.

OUR EXCLUSIVE Summer Camp Tee shirt!

Upon completition they will be rewarded with a special "GOLDEN KEY" because they will have learned the basics of repect and manners that will open many doors in their future!

For everyone's safety it will be agreed upon payment that each PPIE student wears a mask for their safety and the safety of everyone around them. The room will be equip with a hepa-air filtration system and standard COVID cleaning measures will be conducted both before and after each class for everyones safety. PPIE in no way guarantees or accepts responsibility for your child's COVID safety. You are accepting the responsibility of your child's health by choosing to place them in our program. The participants will be seated 3 ft apart for the majority of class however for hands on classroom activities they will be next to one another.

This is an EXCLUSIVE-LIMITED two week summer program ENROLL today!
