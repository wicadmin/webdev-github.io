About

About Our Institute

Premium Paradise Institute of Etiquette provides informative life skill programs designed with a strong curriculum to increase your child's success in todays' diverse, modern and global social society.

Mission

Living in Modern society; our neighbors and friends no longer live next-door, or even in the same town. It is Premium Paradise Institute of Etiquette Mission is to provide the youth and adults of today with the everyday social etiquette tools to meet the modern day societal expectations.

About Marlo Sookraj

Marlo Sookraj is a certified Etiquette Consultant and owner of Premium Paradise Institute of Etiquette. Marlo is a combination of true Americana with the social experience of the melting pot of New York. Her unique insight into many different cultures and religions, and her 10 years of experience working in LCS gives her a broad knowledge of how to introduce the children and professional adults in her community to global etiquette. Living in Lake County FL for 30 yrs, she sees the changes coming to her small town and sees the benefit to offering her unique service to all the Youth, Children, Event Planners and Businesses.
